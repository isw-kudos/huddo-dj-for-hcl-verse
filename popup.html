Auto-change playlist
When on, the DJ switches playlist automatically as your calculated email mood changes.

When off, the mood is still calculated in the background — the circle button will show a coloured ring in the suggested mood colour to prompt you to switch manually.

  • 📬Huddo DJ for HCL Verse watches your HCL Verse inbox and scores it every two minutes based on what it finds.
  • 🔢Unread count — compared against your personal baseline to detect unusual spikes.
  • ⚠️Urgent keywords — subject lines containing words like "urgent", "ASAP", or "deadline" add to the score.
  • 🚩Flagged emails — high-importance messages raise the score further.
  • ⚡Arrival rate — a sudden burst of emails in a short window pushes the mood higher.
  • ⭐VIP senders — emails from contacts you designate below carry extra weight.

The combined score maps to one of five moods, and your DJ picks the right playlist automatically:
